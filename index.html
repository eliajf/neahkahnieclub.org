About the Club

A gathering place for neighbors on the Oregon coast

The Neahkahnie Social Club has been the heart of our beach community for over 70 years. Founded in 1954, we bring together property owners, residents, and friends of the Neahkahnie area near Manzanita, Oregon, for fellowship and fun throughout the year.

We host an annual summer picnic at Neahkahnie Beach, a festive holiday party each December, and gatherings that keep our coastal community connected. Whether you live here year-round or visit when the coast calls, the club is a way to meet your neighbors, share a meal, and be part of something that has endured for generations.

The Neahkahnie Social Club is a 501(c)(7) social club, incorporated as the Neah-Kah-Nie Community Club on August 23, 1954. All are welcome to join.
